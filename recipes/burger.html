Burger

A burger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked,

seasoned patty—typically beef, chicken, or a veggie alternativ,

placed inside a sliced bun. It is commonly customized with cheese,

fresh lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles, and sauces like mayonnaise or ketchup,

often served hot and savory

ingredients

  • Bread Buns(2x)
  • Cabbage
  • Mustard Sauce
  • Potato Patty
  • Cheese

Steps to make a Burger:

  1. Prepare the Patties
  2. Create a Dimple
  3. Chill (Optional)
  4. Season Heavily
  5. Toast the Buns
  6. Cook the Patties
  7. Flip and Cheese
  8. Rest and Assemble
