French Fries

French fries are long, thin strips of potato, typically deep-fried until golden,

crispy on the outside, and soft or fluffy on the inside.

Commonly seasoned with salt, they are a popular,

savory side dish or snack often served with ketchup,

mayonnaise, or other dipping sauces.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Seasonings
  • Oil
  • Tomato sauces

Steps to make French Fries

  1. Cut the Potatoes
  2. Put em' in da oil
  3. fry em up until golden brown
  4. Pair with tomato sauce
  5. Eat!
