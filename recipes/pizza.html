Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flattened,

yeast-leavened bread dough base topped with savory tomato sauce,

mozzarella cheese, and various toppings like vegetables or meats.

It is typically baked at high temperatures, resulting in a crisp crust,

bubbly cheese, and a fragrant, versatile meal loved globally.

Ingredients

  • Cheese (mozzarella)
  • Pizza dough
  • Capscium
  • Pizza sauce
  • Onions
  • Seasonings

Steps to make the pizza:

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Preheat Oven
  3. Shape the Dough
  4. Add Toppings
  5. Bake
  6. Finish and Serve
